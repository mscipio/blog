About me

I'm a biomedical engineer, but also an IT enthusiast currently involved in a PhD program in biomedical engineering at the University of Pisa (UNIPI), Pisa, Italy.

I got my B.Sc. at Marche's Politechnic University (UNIVPM), Ancona, Italy in 2011 and then my M.Sc. the University of Pisa (UNIPI) in 2015 (you can see a short version of my CV clicking here or a longer one here).

Since a couple of months I am member of the Europen Society for Molecular Imaging (ESMI).

My main research topic is about the quantitative analysis of dynamic time series of PET images, with a particular focus on direct kinetic modeling and clustering of raw sinogram data.

Recently, I have presented talks and posters in several international and national conferences:

  • EMIM 2016
  • MEDICON 2016
